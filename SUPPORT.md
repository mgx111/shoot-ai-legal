## Common questions

**My analysis failed / timed out**
Make sure your video is recorded side-on with your full body in frame. Slow internet can also cause timeouts — try again on stronger Wi-Fi.

**My session didn't save / didn't appear on the leaderboard**
Sessions sync to the cloud automatically. Check your internet connection and try Settings → Sync now.

**How do I cancel my Academy subscription?**
On iPhone: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Shoot AI → Cancel.

**How do I delete my account?**
In the app: Settings → Danger zone → Delete account.

**The AI feedback seems off**
Camera angle and clothing matter a lot. Side-on view with your full body in frame and one clean rep per clip gives the best results. The AI reads body landmarks, so anything that hides them (loose clothes, bad lighting) lowers accuracy.

**Refund?**
Refunds are handled by Apple. Open the email receipt for your purchase and tap "Report a Problem", or visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
