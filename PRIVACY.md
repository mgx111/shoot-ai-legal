# Shoot AI Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2 May 2026**

This policy explains what information Shoot AI ("the app", "we", "us") collects when you use it, how that information is used, and what your rights are. Plain language, no legalese.

---

## 1. Who runs Shoot AI

Shoot AI is operated by Max German, a solo developer based in the United Kingdom. You can contact us at **maxgerman07@icloud.com** for any privacy-related question or request.

## 2. What we collect

When you use Shoot AI we collect the following:

**Account information**
- Your email address (used to sign in and recover your account)
- A username you choose
- An anonymous account ID generated by our authentication provider

**Training data**
- Video clips you record or upload, **only for the duration of the AI analysis**. The video is sent to our server, processed, and then **immediately deleted**. We do not store your videos.
- The numeric results of the analysis: overall score, body-position metrics (torso angle, balance, knee angles, etc.), kicking foot detection, and the date of the session
- Your XP, level, daily streak and free-session usage count
- Whether you are on the Free or Academy plan

**Device & technical data**
- Standard server logs (timestamp, request endpoint) needed to keep the service running

We **do not** collect:
- Your real name, address or phone number
- Location data
- Contacts or photos beyond the single video you choose to upload
- Anything from other apps on your device

## 3. How we use the data

- To run the AI shot analysis and return coaching feedback
- To save your session history, XP, streaks, and progress so you can see them across devices
- To rank players on the all-time leaderboard (your username and XP only — never your email)
- To enforce the free-plan weekly upload limit
- To improve the app (general usage patterns — never individual identification)

We do **not** sell your data. We do not share it for advertising. We do not use it to train any AI model.

## 4. Third-party services we rely on

We use a small number of trusted services to make the app work. Each receives only the data needed for its job.

| Service | What they handle | Privacy policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Account login, database storage of your sessions and progress | https://supabase.com/privacy |
| **Anthropic (Claude)** | Generates the coaching feedback text from your numeric session results. **They never receive your video.** | https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy |
| **Railway** | Hosts the analysis server | https://railway.app/legal/privacy |
| **Apple App Store** | Distributes the app and processes any subscription payments | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ |

## 5. Your rights and controls

You can, at any time:

- **See your data** — everything we have about you is visible inside the app (your sessions, XP, leaderboard rank, etc.)
- **Change your username** — Settings → Edit
- **Delete your account and all data** — Settings → Danger zone → Delete account. This permanently removes your sessions, XP, leaderboard entries, and profile from our systems and signs you out.
- **Stop using the app** — uninstalling removes all locally cached data

If for any reason the in-app deletion does not work, email **maxgerman07@icloud.com** with the subject "Delete my data" and we will action it within 7 days.

## 6. How long we keep data

- **Videos:** seconds — deleted immediately after analysis completes
- **Session results, XP, leaderboard:** kept until you delete your account
- **Server logs:** rotated every 30 days

## 7. Children's privacy

Shoot AI is aimed at football players, including those at academy and grassroots level. Some users will be under 18.

- We do **not** knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not create an account.
- If you are 13–17, we recommend a parent or guardian helps set up your account and reviews this policy with you.
- If we discover an account belongs to a child under 13, we will delete it.
- Parents who want a child's account deleted can email **maxgerman07@icloud.com**.

## 8. Security

- All data is sent over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
- Passwords are never stored by us — they are handled by Supabase's authentication system
- Only you can read your own session data

No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect what you share with us.

## 9. International users

The app is operated from the UK and your data may be processed on servers in the United States or European Union (depending on the third-party service). By using the app you consent to this.

## 10. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a meaningful way, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and let you know in the app. Continued use after a change means you accept it.

## 11. Contact

Questions, requests, complaints — all welcome at **maxgerman07@icloud.com**.
